Blueberry Muffins

Description

These blueberry muffins are so good that you can't resist going to sleep and dream about eating them all over again!

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen blueberries
  • All-purpose gluten-free flour
  • Granulated sugar
  • Oil
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Baking powder
  • Salt and vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and a little salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add oil, an egg, milk, and vanilla extract then stir with a fork until blended.
  3. Mix in the blueberries, divide the batter between muffin cups and bake.
