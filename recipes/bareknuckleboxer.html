Bare Knuckle Boxer

Description

Novikov's favorite drink is a rather obscure cocktail known as a "Bare Knuckle Boxer". Nonetheless, it is a drink to die for.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz (45ml) aged rum
  • 0.5 oz (15ml) vodka
  • 4 oz (120ml) orange juice
  • 0.25 oz (10ml) saline solution
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir briefly until chilled.
  3. Strain into an old-fashioned glass.
